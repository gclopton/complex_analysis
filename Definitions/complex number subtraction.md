When you subtract two complex numbers, handle the real parts and imaginary parts separately. Take the real part of the first number and subtract the real part of the second. Then take the imaginary part of the first number and subtract the imaginary part of the second. Finally, combine those results into the new complex number.

$$
(a+i b)-(c+i d)=(a-c)+i(b-d)
$$
