An expression of the from $z + a + ib$, where $a$ and $b$ are real numbers and $i^{2} = -1$. The number $a$ is called the **real part** of $z$ and is denoted $\operatorname{Re} z$. The number $b$ is called the **imaginary part** and is denoted $\operatorname{Im} z$.
